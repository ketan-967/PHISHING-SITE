How It Works

Intelligence at
every click

PhishGuard AI sits silently in your browser, watching every URL you visit. The moment a page loads, it extracts 30 structural features from the URL and sends them to a locally-running FastAPI server.

A trained Random Forest classifier — built on thousands of known phishing and legitimate URLs — scores the threat level in milliseconds. If it's dangerous, you know instantly.
01
URL Intercepted
Chrome's background service worker captures every tab navigation event in real-time.
02
Feature Extraction
30 features extracted: SSL state, URL length, @ symbols, subdomains, dashes, and more.
03
ML Prediction
FastAPI backend runs the Random Forest model, returning a prediction + confidence score.
04
Instant Alert
If phishing or suspicious, a native Chrome notification fires — and the domain is remembered.
